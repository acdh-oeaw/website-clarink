GermanAT is a recognized CLARIN Knowledge Center focussing on the variation, perception, contact and history of German varieties within Austria. Our centre is specialized in capturing different varieties of German in Austria, from dialect to standard varieties. We focus on research from sociolinguistic, quantiative, qualitative, digital and diachronic perspectives.​

​If you have any questions concerning German in Austria or would like our assistance or advice, please feel free to contact us!​

GermanAT is based at the Austrian Centre for Digital Humanities and Cultural Heritage in Cooperation with the Special Research Programme (FWF 060) German in Austria. ​
