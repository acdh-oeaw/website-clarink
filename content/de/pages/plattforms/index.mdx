**Linguae Austriacae: Platform and Information System on Language(s) in Austria (LAPIS)**

The LAPIS (*Linguae Austriacae – Platform and Information System on Language(s) in Austria*) platform currently being developed represents an online-portal which aims to connect different research data and results on language(s) and varieties in Austria. By relying on an overarching data model, LAPIS aims to make heterogenous data from a variety of projects interoperable. Furthermore, LAPIS will offer basic analytical tools (such as corpus queries, visualizations, mapping and other simple quantitative analyses) natively in the browser.

**Lexical Information System Austria (LIÖ)** 

The Lexical Information System Austria (LIÖ) was developed as a cooperation project between the ACDH-CH and the SFB German in Austria. Variation - Contact - Perception (University of Vienna). It is designed as an information and research platform for data on lexical variation in Austria. Currently and initially, LIÖ serves as a central publication medium for all data and content in the context of the WBÖ Project Cluster. This includes in particular the new and retro-digitized dictionary articles as well as the WBÖ document database. The interactive mapping tool can be used to display geographical distributions of dialect data as well as research results from the document database. LIÖ can be found here: [https://wboe.lapis-online.at/](https://wboe.lapis-online.at/)&#x20;

**Atlas on Lexis in Austria in the 21st century (LexAT21)**

LexAT21’s central aim is to comprehensively document lexical variation of the spoken language in Austria visualized through a new mapping tool. By making use of an interactive web application – integrated in the framework of Linguae Austriacae – Platform and Information system on Language(s) in Austria (LAPIS) at the ACDH-CH – lexical variation will be mapped and, thus, visually presented. In a first phase, the joint collaborative project includes online questionnaire data recently elicited online all over the country – an andeavor continued in the future as well. Data presentation in LexAT21 includes the option to individually search, select and map data. In addition, there is access to in-depth descriptions of numerous of the variables (lexical phenomena) elicited, mapped on both areal-horizontal and vertical-social dimensions on the whole dialect-standard-axis. LexAT21, thus, innovatively tackles a central research desideratum in lexis and widens the scope on inter-individual and intra-individual variation at the same time. LexAT21 can be found here: [https://lexat21.lapis-online.at/en](https://lexat21.lapis-online.at/en)&#x20;
