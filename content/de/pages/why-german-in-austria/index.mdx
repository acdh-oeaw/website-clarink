---
title: Why German in Austria?
lead: 'German in Austria: A Sociolinguistic Laboratory'
---
Austria, with its rich dialects, vernaculars and complex dynamics of variation between these and spoken standard represents an ideal laboratory for language scholars concerned with inter- and intraindividual variation.

Austria is traditionally considered to belong to two main dialectal areas, Alemannic in the far west, and Bavarian in the rest of Austria.
