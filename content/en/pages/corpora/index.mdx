**The German in Austria Corpus**

This corpus is a collection of over 600 hours of unmonitored speech, interviews and language production experiments. The corpus is transcribed and partially annotated for POS and dependencies. For more information, see [https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/resources/corpora/dioe-corpus](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/resources/corpora/dioe-corpus).

**The Austrian Media Corpus**

The Austrian Media Corpus is a collection of journalistic prose covering almost the entire Austrian media landscape over the last decade, consisting of over 12 billion token. The corpus is annotated for POS, dependencies and media types as well as areas in Austria. For more information, see [https://amc.acdh.oeaw.ac.at/](https://amc.acdh.oeaw.ac.at/).&#x20;

**The L1-Long Corpus**

Thew L1-Long corpus is a corpus based on three monolingual Viennese children, encompassing 98 hours of material in total. The corpus is tagged for POS and inflectional morphology and is transcribed in CHAT format. For more information, see [https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/resources/corpora/l1-long-corpus](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/resources/corpora/l1-long-corpus).&#x20;

**The Austrian Dialect in the 20th Century Corpus**

This corpus is part of a cooperation project of the ACDH-CH´s Research Unit Linguistics and the Phonogrammarchiv of the ÖAW. It consists of a collection of approximately 2450 recordings of Austrian dialects that were compiled from the 1950ties to the 1980ties in the context of the WBÖ in cooperation with the Phonogrammarchiv of the ÖAW. For more information, see [https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/resources/corpora/corpus-austrian-dialect-recordings-in-the-20th-century](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/resources/corpora/corpus-austrian-dialect-recordings-in-the-20th-century).&#x20;
