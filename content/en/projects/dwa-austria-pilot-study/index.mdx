The pilot study *DWA Austria* is a cooperative project of the Research Unit Linguistics at the ACDH, the *SFB German in Austria. Variation - Contact - Perception* and the *DSA | Research Center Deutscher Sprachatlas* at the Philipps University of Marburg. As part of the pilot study, the possibilities of AI-supported recording of DWA questionnaires are being tested. On the basis of the pilot study, the project aims to completely digitize all Austrian DWA questionnaires and carries out extensive analyses of the data.

The pilot study has resulted in the research project LexVAD20, funded by the FWF.&#x20;

For further information, visit the [project website](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/research/corpus-linguistics/dwa-austria-pilot-study).&#x20;
