---
title: Language Dynamics in Austria (DynAT)
lead: >-
  The project Language Dynamics in Austria (DynAT) is a long-term project of the
  Austrian Academy of Sciences (ÖAW). The aim of the project is to document and
  analyse processes of language variation and language change in Austria. 
---
The activities in the project have a particular focus on the following research areas:

* Language Corpora & Infrastructure
* Language Variation and Language Change
* Lexicography
* Science Communication

For further information, visit the [project website](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/dynat).&#x20;
