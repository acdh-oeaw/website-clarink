By making use of an interactive web application – integrated in the framework of Linguae Austriacae – Platform and Information system on Language(s) in Austria (LAPIS) at the ACDH – lexical variation will be mapped and, thus, visually presented. In a first phase, the joint collaborative project includes online questionnaire data recently elicited online all over the country – an andeavor continued in the future as well. Data presentation in LexAT21 includes the option to individually search, select and map data. In addition, there is access to in-depth descriptions of numerous of the variables (lexical phenomena) elicited, mapped on both areal-horizontal and vertical-social dimensions on the whole dialect-standard-axis. LexAT21, thus, innovatively tackles a central research desideratum in lexis and widens the scope on inter-individual and intra-individual variation at the same time.

For further information, visit the [project website](https://lexat21.lapis-online.at/en).&#x20;
