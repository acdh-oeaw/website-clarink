The project’s central objective is to make historical dialect data from the 20th century accessible, as well as to carry out geolinguistic mapping and analytical investigations of these materials. This data was collected around 85 years ago in the context of the Marburg research project *German Word Atlas* / *Deutscher Wortatlas*. As part of this project, questionnaires were disseminated to around 50,000 schools in German-speaking countries and regions, with the exception of Switzerland. The task was to request the translation of the words on the questionnaires and a few complete sentences into the dialect of the school location by the teachers. The data from the questionnaire formed the basis for the *German Word Atlas* / *Deutscher Wortatlas* (1951–1980), which comprises a total of 22 volumes of black and white dialect maps which are, however, quite difficult to read.

For further information, visit the [project website](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/research/language-dynamics/lexvad20).&#x20;
