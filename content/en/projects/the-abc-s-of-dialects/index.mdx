The project aims at transcribing and evaluating the handwriting and lexis found on the over 100-year-old paper slips of the Dictionary of Bavarian Dialects in Austria (WBÖ). The goal is to engage citizens with their linguistic and regional heritage, making the historical information digitally accessible, and connecting to current research on lexis and language variation and change in the DiÖ project.

Citizens are guided and trained in the transcription, with citizen experts and academic researchers ensuring the validation process. The scans of the paper slips and the obtained knowledge is made available via Zooniverse, a well-known platform for citizen science activities.

For further information, visit the [project website](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/acdh/research/linguistics/research/corpus-linguistics/the-abcs-of-dialects).&#x20;
